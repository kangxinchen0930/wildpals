About WildPals

Less chat chaos. More real sessions.

WildPals helps groups organise sport more clearly — and makes it easier for people to join.

Why it exists

Too many chats

Plans get buried, details go missing, and nobody knows who is actually in.

Too much admin

Organisers end up doing all the chasing instead of running the session.

Too much friction

Joining should feel simple, especially for people outside the main group chat.

How it works

STEP 01

Create

Post the sport, time, place, and details.

STEP 02

Join

Members see what is on and request to join.

STEP 03

Go

Everyone sees a clear plan. Less confusion, better turnout.
