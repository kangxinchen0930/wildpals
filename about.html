About WildPals

WildPals exists to make group sport sessions actually happen — without the chaos of group chats, spreadsheets, and last-minute dropouts.

What we are building

WildPals is a simple home for clubs and friend groups to organise sessions (running, cycling, climbing) and for members to request to join. Clear details, fewer cancellations, less admin — and stronger community.

For organisers

  • Create sessions fast (sport, time, location, level)
  • Member requests instead of endless DMs
  • Clear activity details everyone can see
  • Less admin, more sport

For members

  • See what is on at a glance
  • Request to join in one tap
  • Know exactly when/where/what to expect
  • Safety tools: report & block (built in)

Why now

Clubs and crews run on WhatsApp — but it breaks at scale. Plans stay vague, people drop late, and organisers do all the chasing. WildPals turns “maybe” into a clear plan: who is in, what is happening, and where to meet — with the foundations for smarter matching over time.

Beta

We are opening a small beta for the first version and would love help testing. If you run sessions or regularly join group training, you are exactly who we want to hear from.
